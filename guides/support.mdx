## Overview

Hive provides a built-in contact system to help you reach your organization's administrator when you need assistance. The contact feature is accessible from the sidebar navigation and displays the administrator's contact information.

## Accessing Support

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Contact page">
    Click **Contacto** in the sidebar navigation to access the contact screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View contact information">
    You'll see the administrator's details including:
    - Phone number
    - Email address
    - Office address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get in touch">
    Use the provided contact information to reach out via phone, email, or in person.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact Modal (Quick Access)

For quick access to contact information, especially when not logged in:

1. Click the **Contactar al administrador** button in the top navigation
2. A modal will appear with the administrator's contact details
3. Click outside the modal or press the **X** button to close it

<Info>
The contact modal is primarily designed for users who need help before logging in or when they don't have full access to the application.
</Info>

### Sending an Email

Click the **Enviar correo** button on the contact page to open your default email client with:
- Pre-filled recipient (administrator's email)
- Subject line: "Contacto - Hive"

This makes it easy to send a properly formatted support request.

## When to Contact Support

Contact your administrator for:

- **Technical issues** - Login problems, access errors, or system bugs
- **Account management** - Password resets, permission requests, or role changes
- **Feature requests** - Suggestions for new functionality or improvements
- **Training** - Questions about how to use specific features
- **Administrative tasks** - User management, project setup, or organization settings

<Tip>
Before contacting support, check the documentation for answers to common questions. This can help you resolve issues faster.
</Tip>
